company profile

Situated at Funing Hi-tech Industry Park, Yancheng City, Jiangsu Province, Jiangsu Lynwon Technology Co., Ltd. is a national high-tech enterprise covering 330,000 square meters of place. As the world’s leading supplier for fine chemicals, with over 25 years of rich experience in production, Lynwon Technology has been providing its clients with an extensive selection of Lynwon branded products, including inks, plastics, paints, etc. Lynwon’s full collection of products offer over 200 varieties, including AZO pigment, phthalocyanine pigment, quinacridone pigment, fluorescent pigment, waterborne paste and pigment intermediate.

Lynwon Technology holds environmental protection in great value by producing green products. While creating a vivid life so rich in color, Lynwon never forgets about protecting the green and healthy environment. Lynwon’s products have been INTERTEK certified, meeting various international safety requirements and regulations, including REACH, PAHs, RoHs, EN71-3, etc. Lynwon’s efforts for environmental protection lies within technology. The company’s 40,000 sqm sewage disposal system was created from an USD 12,000,000 investment, which helps the company to obtain ISO 9001 Product Quality System Certification, ISO 14001 Environmental Protection System Certification, and OHSAS 18001 Occupation Health System Certification.

Lywnon Technology has been and will be always dedicated in developing, producing and optimizing greater solutions for our clients. And we look forward to helping you to conquer new challenges and being your reliable partner!
